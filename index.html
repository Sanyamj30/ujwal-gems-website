Our Heritage

Jaipur has been the beating heart of the world's gemstone trade for over 500 years. Known as the "Gem Capital of the World", the Pink City is home to generations of master craftsmen whose hands have shaped raw stones — plucked from the earth in distant mines — into treasures of extraordinary beauty. This centuries-old tradition of lapidary artistry, passed down through families and guilds, gives Jaipur an unrivalled depth of knowledge and skill that no modern facility can replicate.

At Ujwal Gems, we honour that legacy through the way we source. We maintain direct relationships with miners in Zambia's Copperbelt, Afghanistan's Panjshir Valley, and Colombia's legendary Muzo region — the three origins that define the finest emeralds on earth. By working at the source, we ensure every stone that reaches our workshop is ethically sourced, naturally untreated, and accompanied by internationally recognised certification from GIA, IGI, or a trusted Jaipur laboratory. What you receive is not just a gemstone; it is a provenance story you can trust.

Gemstone Vault

Zambian Emerald

Mined in Zambia's Copperbelt, these emeralds are prized for their deep, bluish-green hue and exceptional clarity. The cooler tones and high transparency make Zambian stones among the most sought-after in the world. Each stone is hand-selected for colour saturation and natural character.

Panjshir Emerald

Sourced from Afghanistan's legendary Panjshir Valley, these emeralds display a vivid, pure green with remarkable transparency. Panjshir stones are relatively new to the international market, yet their exceptional colour and minimal inclusions have already earned them a prestigious reputation among collectors and connoisseurs.

Colombian Emerald

The benchmark against which all emeralds are measured, Colombian stones from the Muzo and Chivor mines possess a warm, intensely saturated green that is instantly recognisable. Revered for centuries by royalty and collectors alike, Colombian emeralds represent the pinnacle of gemstone heritage and remain the world's most coveted origin.

Certified Quality

Every emerald from Ujwal Gems is accompanied by a certificate from a recognised gemological authority, verifying its natural origin, weight, and quality.
